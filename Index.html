About Us

We are a registered family and self development learning organisation established in 2014. We have seen great positive results over the years and restored many relationships. Our programs are presented by zandile
chiloane, a personal and self development coach. We face so many
challenges and anxieties in life, which disturbs our inner peace or our relationships with others. Our organisation primary goal is to restore
peace and stability within self and with others. We provide one on one or
group training and team activities that stimulate self-development. You
will learn skills to equip you deal with ongoing daily challenges.
